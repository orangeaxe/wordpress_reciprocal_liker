# Frequently Asked Questions

## What does Reciprocal Liker do?

It queues reciprocal likes for WordPress.com users who have liked your posts.

---

## Does it instantly like posts?

No.

Likes are delayed using a randomized schedule.

---

## What is Dry Run?

Dry Run simulates all actions without liking anything.

Use Dry Run when testing.

---

## Will it like my own posts?

No.

The plugin automatically ignores your own site.

---

## Will it comment on posts?

No.

The plugin only queues and processes likes.

---

## Will it repost content?

No.

The plugin never republishes content.

---

## Why are some users skipped?

Possible reasons:

- Private site
- No public posts available
- WordPress.com API restrictions
- OAuth target mismatch

---

## What does "Could not obtain a reciprocation target" mean?

The plugin detected a liker but could not retrieve a valid public post to like.

No action is taken.

---

## Does it work with WordPress.org sites?

Only if the site participates through WordPress.com services accessible through the API.

Results vary.

---

## Can I control delays?

Yes.

Set a minimum and maximum delay in the plugin settings.

Example:

1 minute to 180 minutes

The plugin will randomly select a time within that range.
